# Changing the Delta Icon
> :warning: NOTICE: The icon will RESET every update.
## Android Tutorial
1. Download an image that will serve as the new icon. It should be a square or circle with a background.
2. Join a Roblox game.
3. Wait until the Delta UI loads. Exit out of Roblox afterwards.
4. Open ZArchiver. If you do not already have it, install it from the Play Store.
5. Go to 0/storage/emulated.
6. Click on "Download".
7. Scroll until you find the icon you downloaded.
8. Hold it. fterwards, click "Copy".
9. Swipe back, then click on the Delta folder.
10. Click on "Workspace".
11. Click the clipboard icon at the bottom right.
12. Delete "new_logo.png".
13. Rename the icon you added to "new_logo.png" (hold it, then click Rename, then click Ok).
14. That should be all!

## iOS Tutorial
1. Join a Roblox game.
2. Wait until the Delta UI loads. Exit out of Roblox afterwards.
3. Find the image you want to use. Hold it, then click "Copy".
4. Open the Files app.
5. Go to Browse > On My iPhone/iPad > Delta > Delta > Workspace.
6. Hold your screen (NOT ON A FILE), then click "Paste".
7. Hold new_logo.png, then click "Delete".
8. Rename the file you pasted to new_logo.png.
