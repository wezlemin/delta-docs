# Autoexecuting on iOS
> Android Tutorial (using ZArchiver): [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dddPBPXdvpU](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dddPBPXdvpU)

## Instructions
> In Delta's settings (cog icon), make sure "Auto Execute" is enabled!
1. Open a game in Delta. If you have not already, complete the keysystem.
2. Click the icon of the house in the Delta GUI.
3. Click Upload, then enter a title for the script.
4. Enter the script. It should be a loadstring.
5. Open the Files app on your device.
6. Go to Browse > On My (Device) > Delta > Delta > Scripts.
7. Hold the script you made, then click Move.
8. Click Browse > On My (Device) > Delta > Delta > Autoexecute. Click Move.
9. You're done!
