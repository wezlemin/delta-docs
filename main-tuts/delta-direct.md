# Installing Delta via Direct Install
1. Delete Delta Roblox if you already have it. Turn off your VPN if you have one enabled.
2. Go to [https://delta.bz/](https://delta.bz/) and click on Direct Install.
3. Go to your home screen and the Delta app should start downloading. 
4. Once Delta finishes downloading, click on it. If you get an _integrity_ error, check the "Notes" section.
5. Go to System Settings → VPN and Device Management. Trust the Delta app and restart your device.
6. You're done! Whenever you get a pop-up asking you to complete two tasks, just click the two blue buttons and wait 30 seconds; the pop-up will disappear.

## Notes
- Direct Install is managed by a third-party ([Skibidi Tech](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCcBIs4ID-Tg6l48h0a-axxw)). This means that updates can take longer.
- If you get upgrade/integrity errors, uninstall Delta and reinstall it. If you still get upgrade/integrity errors, sign and install using the Delta IPA
  or wait for Skibidi Tech to update Direct. He usually makes a video whenever he updates/changes the certificate used for Delta,
  so subscribe to him and keep your notifications (to his channel) on.
