What is Delta?​

Delta is a free developer tool for Roblox that can run most available Roblox Luau scripts. It supports Android and iOS devices natively, as well as computers via emulators such as MuMuPlayer and BlueStacks. Delta is not a virus, but it may be flagged by security tools such as VirusTotal or Triage as a Trojan due to its behavior.

It is usually undetected, meaning the risk of a Roblox account ban is low under normal circumstances. However, you can still be banned from specific games, especially if you use detected scripts that trigger a game's anti-cheat or honeypots.

For this reason, it is recommended to use an alternative (alt) account. Additionally, during certain periods (such as Roblox events) or if Roblox updates its anti-cheat, Delta itself may become detected, which can result in a platform-wide ban.

Scripts for Delta can be found on ScriptBlox. You can also find scripts in YouTube videos, as well as through Delta’s built-in Script Explorer (the cloud icon in its UI).

While exploiting itself is not illegal, it does violate Roblox’s terms of service. As a result, you may be banned from individual games or from Roblox entirely for using scripts, as mentioned earlier.
