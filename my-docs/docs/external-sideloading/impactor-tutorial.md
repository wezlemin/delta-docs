# Installing Delta via Impactor

**Official Links:**
> Impactor Website: [https://impactor.khcrysalis.dev/](https://impactor.khcrysalis.dev/) <br />
> Impactor Docs: [https://impactor.khcrysalis.dev/docs/introduction/](https://impactor.khcrysalis.dev/docs/introduction/) <br />
> Impactor GitHub: [https://github.com/CLARATION/Impactor](https://github.com/CLARATION/Impactor)

## Requirements
- Mac running macOS 10.13 High Sierra or later OR a Windows computer running Windows 10 or later OR a computer running Linux
- iPhone/iPad with iOS 9 or higher
- Apple ID & iCloud aged 13+

## Instructions
1. Connect your computer to your iDevice (iPhone/iPad).
2. Go to the Impactor website.
3. Click on Download and click the relevant download for your computer. Afterwards, install it.
4. Download the Delta IPA from [https://deltaexploits.gg](https://deltaexploits.gg).
5. Open Impactor and click Import .ipa / .tipa.
6. Find and select the Delta IPA you downloaded.
7. Make sure your iDevice is selected at the top right, then click Install.
8. Enter your Apple ID (email) and Apple ID password.
9. Click Next, then wait for it to log-in.
10. On your phone, click the "Apple Account Sign In Required" pop-up, then unlock your iDevice.
11. Click Allow, then enter and verify the 6 digit code it shows into the Two-Factor Authenticator box Impactor shows.
12. Wait a few minutes. Make sure your phone is unlocked.
13. On your iDevice, Delta should have downloaded. Go to Settings, VPN and Device Management, trust the app, then enable developer mode in Privacy and Security (iOS 16+).
14. After restarting, you should be able to open Delta!

**⚠️ You must use Impactor to sideload the IPA again every time Delta updates or when 7 days have passed since your last sign. This is because with a free Apple ID, each signed app expires after 7 days, and Impactor can only re-sign via your computer.**
