# Installing Delta via AppleJr
1. Delete your current version of Delta Roblox. Turn your VPN off if you have one on.
2. Go to https://applejr.net (WARNING: THERE ARE LOADS OF POPUPS).
3. Click on Signer and click “(AppleJr DNS Required)”. Click Allow for the configuration profile.
4. Go to System Settings → General → VPN and Device Management. Click Install on the DNS profile, enter your password when prompted, and click Install again to confirm.
5. Download the Delta IPA by going to https://deltaexploits.gg. Use Safari.
6. Go back to AppleJr and click “Click here to Upload IPA”. Select the Delta IPA, choose a certificate from the list, and click Sign IPA. Wait for it to sign (it takes quite a while).
7. Click Install IPA and click Install when the popup appears.
8. Wait for the app to install. Click on Delta and make sure it does not give an integrity error (if it does, try resigning with a different certificate).
9. Go to System Settings → General → VPN and Device Management. Trust the enterprise app and restart your device.
10. Click Delta; you’re done!
