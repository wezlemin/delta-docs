# Adding Delta Assets Manually
This is for when they refuse to load on **iOS**. If you are on **Android**, use a VPN (such as ProtonVPN or Cloudflare WARP) to load the assets, then turn it off afterwards. <br>
**DO NOT USE A VPN ON IOS OR YOUR CERTIFICATE MIGHT GET REVOKED, RESULTING IN DELTA CRASHING!**

## Instructions
1. Download [this](https://github.com/wezlemin/delta-docs/raw/refs/heads/main/general/Workspace.zip) ZIP.
2. Check the Downloads section for Safari (top-left or bottom-left).
3. Click on Workspace, then click the Share icon.
4. Click Save to Files.
5. Click on Browse > On My (Device) > Delta > Delta > click Save.
6. Open the Files app on your device.
7. Go to Browse > On My (Device) > Delta > Delta.
8. Delete the old Workspace folder (hold it and then press Delete).
9. Click on the Workspace ZIP. It will create a Delta folder.
10. Delete the Workspace ZIP. You're done!
